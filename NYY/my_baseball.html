The New York Yankees

This website will be about the New York Yankees and why they are one of the best teams in baseball.

Who are the New York Yankees?

The New York Yankees are a baseball team that is based out of the Bronx of New York and are part of the AL East division. They Yankees are owned by a man named Harold Steinbrenner who inharated the team from his father before him who passed away in 2010.

Yankee Legends

There have been many Yankee legends througout the time period of baseball, but there are a few well known players that really stood out the most to many people. These players include:

  1. Alex Rodriguez who was the third basemen for the yankees from 2004-2016.
  2. Reggie Jackson who was nicknamed "Mr. October" played in the outfield for the yankees from 1977-1981.
  3. Whitey Ford who was a pitcher for the yankees from 1950-1967.
  4. Derek Jeter who was arguably the best shortstop the yankees ever had from 1995-2014.
  5. Babe Ruth who was an outfielder and pitcher for the yankees and was possibly the best yankee to ever exist played from 1920-1934.
  6. Mickey Mantle who was an outfielder for the yankees from 1951-1968.
  7. Yogi Berra who was one of the greatest catchers in baseball history played for the yankees from 1946-1963.
  8. Mariano Rivera who has the record for the most all time saves played for the yankees from 1995-2013.
